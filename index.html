More About Nature Conservation Day

⭐ Why Is Nature Conservation Day Celebrated ⭐

It is celebrated on July 28 annually. The main purpose of celebrating World Nature Conservation Day is to conserve the animals and trees that are on the verge of going extinct from the natural environment of the Earth. Conservation of resources has an important role in protecting our mother Earth.


⭐ Four Ways to Celebrate World Nature Conservation Day ⭐

1. Volunteer in your community’s environmental programs

2. Support conservation organizations through donating

3. Stick by the 3 R’s (Reduce, Reduce, Recycle) in your everyday life

4. Stay in the know, educate yourself, and spread awareness


⭐ When did World Environment Day begin? ⭐

Led by the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) and held annually on 5 June since 1973, World Environment Day is the largest global platform for environmental public outreach and is celebrated by millions of people across the world.
